Course Overview:

This course will focus on the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals and how they impact and relate to our lives here in El Salvador. Students will become aware, or dive deeper into their awareness of the major issues of the day. They will have an in depth understanding and appreciation of current events around the world. The focus of the class will be issues that affect the student as residents of the World and El Salvador. The class will follow daily news events and will be expected to understand the social, political and economic issues on a daily basis. A new SDG will be covered every two weeks. Students will get to choose their SDG every third topic.
